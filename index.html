Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2026

Flux ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.

The App may store limited non-personal data locally on your device, such as:

  • Cached currency exchange rates
  • App preferences (e.g., theme settings)

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

2. Internet Access

The App requires an internet connection to retrieve currency exchange rates from third-party APIs. These services may collect anonymous technical data as part of their operation.

3. Third-Party Services

The App may rely on third-party services to provide currency data. These services operate under their own privacy policies.

4. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected, there is minimal risk related to personal data exposure.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
